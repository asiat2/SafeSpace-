# 🧘 SafeSpace

## Overview

SafeSpace is a Flutter-based mental wellness application designed to provide users with simple, accessible tools for relaxation, stress management, and emotional self-awareness.

The application offers guided breathing exercises, panic relief support, mood tracking, and calming nature sounds in a clean and user-friendly interface. SafeSpace was developed to promote mindfulness and emotional well-being while providing users with immediate support during moments of stress or anxiety.

---

## Problem Statement

Stress, anxiety, and emotional overwhelm are increasingly common in daily life. Many individuals need quick and accessible tools that can help them calm down, regulate their emotions, and practice mindfulness without requiring complex setup or professional intervention.

SafeSpace addresses this need by offering:

- Immediate panic relief guidance
- Guided breathing exercises
- Mood reflection tools
- Relaxing ambient sounds
- Mindfulness support resources

---

## Features

### ❤️ Panic Relief

The Panic Relief module provides immediate emotional support during stressful moments.

Features include:

- Calming reassurance messages
- Grounding techniques
- 5-4-3-2-1 sensory awareness exercise
- Encouragement to focus on the present moment

This feature helps users regain control and reduce feelings of panic or overwhelm.

---

### 🌬️ Guided Breathing Exercise

The breathing exercise feature helps users slow their breathing and improve relaxation.

Features include:

- Animated breathing guide
- Visual inhale and exhale prompts
- Session lengths:
  - 1 minute
  - 3 minutes
  - 5 minutes
- Start, pause, and stop controls
- Session completion notification

Benefits:

- Reduced stress
- Improved focus
- Better emotional regulation
- Mindfulness practice

---

### 😊 Mood Check

The Mood Check module encourages users to reflect on their emotional state.

Available mood selections:

- Great
- Good
- Okay
- Sad
- Anxious
- Overwhelmed

Each mood provides supportive and encouraging feedback to help users acknowledge and process their feelings.

---

### 🎵 Relax Sounds

The Relax Sounds feature provides calming audio experiences designed to promote relaxation.

Available sounds:

- Rain
- Ocean Waves
- Forest Ambience
- White Noise

Features:

- Play audio
- Pause audio
- Stop audio
- Sound status display

These sounds can be used during meditation, studying, relaxation, or sleep preparation.
